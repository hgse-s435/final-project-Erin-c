CSNYC Education Meetup Tuesday, November 12 6:00pm - 8:30pm @ Turn to Tech Attend the inaugural education meetup organized by The NYC Foundation for Computer Science Education (CSNYC). Scratch, Java, Robots, Web? We're coming together to build a community of professional practice around computer science education in New York City. All invested community members are welcome. We are working to bring in national level speakers, find fun and dynamic venues and build a space for sharing ideas and best practices in the rapidly growing world of computer science. Join us for monthly workshops, master classes, and whatever else we can come up with. See you there, and bring friends! Sign up at http://www.meetup.com/CSNYC-Education-Meetup/events/147094432
