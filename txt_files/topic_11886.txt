The biennial Scratch@MIT Conferencewill be heldfrom July 25 - July 28, 2012. Hosted by the ScratchEd Team at the MIT Media Lab, the conference is a wonderful opportunity for those working with Scratch (in the classroom, in informal settings, in research, or other)to share their experienceswithothers. The theme for this year's conference is Create Your World. Proposal submissions are now being accepted until Wednesday, February 15. You can propose a poster/demo, presentation, panel discussion, workshop, or suggest something else. Create your world!
