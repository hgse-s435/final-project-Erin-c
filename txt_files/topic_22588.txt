ScratchEd Weekly Roundup 09/14/13 - 09/20/13 The ScratchEd Team will be hosting monthly Scratch educator meetups again this academic year starting October 5. Join us every first Saturday at MIT Media Lab to network, share, and learn with and from other local Boston educators.
