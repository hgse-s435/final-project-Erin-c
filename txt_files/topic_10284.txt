ScratchEd Weekly Roundup 07/23/11 - 07/29/11 The Scratch Team has been doing some work on the Scratch website this week. We apologize for any hiccups. -http://scratch.mit.edu/ Thanks to everyone who joined theScratchEd Webinaron Monday to discuss ScratchEd Resources.
